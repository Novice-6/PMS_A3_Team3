1. Scope & Application

This Privacy & Security Policy applies to all users of the Inventory Management Application, governing the collection, usage, storage, and protection of all data generated and processed within the platform. This policy is formulated in compliance with educational data security standards and mobile application development specifications.

2. Data Protection & Security

We implement end-to-end secure transmission protocols and strict data encryption mechanisms to protect all inventory and operational data. Multi-layer validation and access control rules are established to prevent unauthorized access, tampering, disclosure, or destruction of any information within the application.

3. User Privacy Commitment

The application strictly upholds a zero-collection principle for personal identifiable information (PII), including but not limited to user identities, locations, device data, and private metadata. No personal information is collected, stored, or shared with any third party at any time.

4. Data Usage Principles

All data entered and processed in this application is used **exclusively for educational demonstration and course assessment** under the PROG2005 program. No data is used for commercial, marketing, or unauthorized purposes, ensuring full compliance with academic ethical standards.

5. Data Storage & Retention

Inventory data is temporarily stored for functional demonstration only and will not be permanently preserved beyond the scope of the project. Critical system data is protected from accidental deletion or modification to maintain data integrity and application stability.

6. Application Security Standards

The application integrates complete form validation, value range restrictions, and operation confirmation dialogs to avoid invalid inputs and unexpected data loss. All interactive modules follow global best practices for secure mobile application design and development.

7. Policy Update & Notification

We reserve the right to update this policy to adapt to new functional requirements or regulatory standards. Any revisions will take effect immediately upon application update without prior separate notification.

8. Disclaimer & Liability

This application is a student academic project for learning and assessment only, not for commercial or production use. The development team shall not be liable for any data loss, service interruptions, or technical issues arising from the use of this application.
