1 Scope & Application

This Privacy & Security Policy applies to all users of the Inventory Management Application, governing the collection, usage, storage, and protection of all data generated and processed within the platform.

2 Data Protection & Security

We implement secure data handling and strict validation rules to protect all inventory information. Multi-layer access control prevents unauthorized access, tampering, or data loss.

3 User Privacy Commitment

This application collects zero personal data. No user accounts, passwords, or device information are stored. All operations remain fully anonymous.

4 Data Usage Principles

All inventory data is used only for app functionality and academic assessment. No data is shared, sold, or used for commercial purposes.

5 Data Storage & Retention

All data is stored locally and never transmitted to external servers. Temporary storage ensures demonstration functionality without permanent tracking.

6 Application Security Standards

Full form validation, unique item checks, auto-increment IDs, and clear error handling ensure stable, secure, and predictable application behavior.

7 Policy Update

This policy may be updated to meet academic requirements. Changes take effect immediately upon app update.

8 Disclaimer & Liability

This is a student academic project for PROG2005 assessment only. Not for commercial use. Developers are not liable for data loss or service issues.
